One-line: User/account avatar with initials fallback, and a hairline `Separator`.

```jsx
<Avatar initials="BB" />
<Avatar src="/me.png" alt="Bobi" size="lg" />
<Separator />
<Separator orientation="vertical" />
```

Avatar `size`: `xs`/`sm`/`md`/`lg` or a number (px). Initials are mono and uppercased.
