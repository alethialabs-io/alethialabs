One-line: The bordered surface that holds clusters, vines, integrations, and cost summaries.

```jsx
<Card interactive>
  <CardHeader>
    <CardTitle>api-backend</CardTitle>
    <CardDescription>EKS · eu-west-1 · v1.31</CardDescription>
  </CardHeader>
  <CardBody>…</CardBody>
  <CardFooter><Button variant="outline" size="sm">Open</Button></CardFooter>
</Card>
```

Compose with `CardHeader`, `CardTitle`, `CardDescription`, `CardBody`, `CardFooter`. Use `interactive` for clickable cards, `flat` to drop the shadow inside dense layouts.
