The Margin Risk · Contained

AI is the only variable cost — and it's bounded.

Included AI credits are generous. Fully consumed on the premium model, the allowance alone is a large share of plan revenue — so we engineer it down, and it can never run away.

Pricing Decision · Team Tier

$29 / seat — with a per-org floor.

Per-seat pricing captures team value, but a single-seat org is the one structurally-negative corner. A small seat minimum removes it without re-pricing the shipped plan.

Decision: keep the shipped $29/seat, add a 3-seat (~$87) minimum. Margin turns positive at two seats and compounds from there.
