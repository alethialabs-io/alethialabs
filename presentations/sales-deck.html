Live demo · the hero flow

Straight answers

The questions you're about to ask.

QuestionAnswer
Is this lock-in?No. You get real EKS + ArgoCD + standard Kustomize/Helm, wired to your repo. There's nothing proprietary to be trapped in — leaving means nothing, because it's already yours.
Do you hold our keys?Never. The runner runs in your account and assumes scoped, short-lived roles at execution time. The control plane never sees or stores cloud credentials. Pluggable-provider secrets flow to the runner at runtime only — never in the config snapshot.
Can we self-host?Yes — the entire control plane self-hosts as ~4 containers (Postgres + S3 + app + runner), AGPL-licensed. Run it air-gapped, in-region, under your own audit. No Supabase, no SaaS dependency.
Is it real Terraform / real k8s?Yes. The Spec generates real OpenTofu you own and a real managed Kubernetes cluster. Not an abstraction over Terraform you can't see — the actual artifacts, in your account.
