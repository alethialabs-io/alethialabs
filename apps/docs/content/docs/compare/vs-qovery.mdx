# Alethia vs Qovery

**Qovery** is a BYOC Kubernetes control plane / app-PaaS — connect a repo + your cloud, and it
provisions managed K8s in your account and deploys into it (recently repositioned around
"Kubernetes for humans **and AI agents**"). It's the closest structural analog to Alethia; the
divergence is **ownership and the credential model**.

| Dimension | Alethia | Qovery |
|---|---|---|
| Control plane | **Self-hostable** (~4 containers, AGPL) | Qovery-hosted SaaS by default; self-host only on Enterprise |
| Credential model | **Runner assumes roles at run time; control plane holds nothing** | Hosted control plane is a privileged orchestrator into your account (even with STS) |
| App delivery | **Real ArgoCD wired to your repo** (portable) | Qovery's engine *is* the delivery layer (remove it → rebuild your pipeline) |
| Provisioned stack | Cluster **+ Aurora/ElastiCache/DynamoDB/SQS-SNS/ECR/S3/Secrets/Route53/WAF** in one Project | App-platform-centric; most managed data services left to you |
| Pricing | Free self-host; commercial for orgs/SSO | Per-org + seats + deploy-minutes; self-host gated to Enterprise |
| Open source | **AGPL core** | Proprietary |

## Where Alethia wins
- **Truly credential-free** — the control plane never holds privileged access; Qovery's hosted
  brain is always a privileged orchestrator into your account.
- **Self-host on day one, for free** — AGPL, ~4 containers. Qovery gates self-host behind Enterprise.
- **Portable app-delivery** — real ArgoCD; delete Alethia and your cluster + GitOps keep running.
- **Broader stack in one Project** + no per-seat / per-deploy-minute meter.

## Where Qovery wins (honest)
- **Multi-cloud breadth GA** (EKS/GKE/AKS/Scaleway + bring-your-own clusters) today.
- **Mature platform** — well-funded, established, with a polished console and the new
  AI-agent guardrails/policy story.
- **Managed app-platform UX** is further along than Alethia's V1 dashboard.

<Callout type="info">
Bottom line: Qovery is the more mature managed control plane. Choose Alethia when "the control
plane must be ours and hold no credentials" is a hard requirement.
</Callout>
